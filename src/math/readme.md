## LaTeX Examples

Common mathematical notations in LaTeX:

1. Fractions: \\( \frac{a}{b} \\)
2. Summation: \\( \sum_{i=1}^n x_i \\)
3. Integrals: \\( \int_a^b f(x) dx \\)
4. Limits: \\( \lim_{x \to \infty} f(x) \\)
5. Matrices: \\( \begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \\)
